About experience

At Sunshine Resort, you can enjoy a truly unforgettable vacation experience. Relax on the pristine sandy beaches and soak up the sun while taking in the stunning ocean views. Take a dip in the crystal-clear waters, or indulge in a variety of water sports such as snorkeling or jet skiing. The resort's on-site restaurants offer a delicious array of local cuisine, while the spa provides a tranquil retreat for guests looking to unwind with a massage or facial. Stay active with the resort's tennis courts, fitness center, and yoga classes, or explore nearby attractions such as historic sites and nature reserves. As the sun begins to set, embark on a sunset cruise and take in the breathtaking views. In the evening, enjoy live music and dancing or simply relax and take in the peaceful atmosphere.
